Privacy Policy for Malume App

Effective Date: June 17, 2025

Malume does not collect or share any personal information directly. The app connects users (parents and drivers) to track school bus trips in real time using GPS data.

What We Collect

  • Device Location: Only used for tracking the school bus location during trips.
  • User Input: Names and contact info if entered by parents to receive notifications.

Data Sharing

Data is never shared with third parties. It is only used for the purpose of bus tracking within the app.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for use by parents and drivers. While it may involve data about children (e.g., pickup/drop-off info), no child accesses the app directly.

Data Retention

Trip and location data may be stored for up to 30 days to improve service reliability, after which it is deleted.

Contact

If you have questions, contact us at support@malume.app.
